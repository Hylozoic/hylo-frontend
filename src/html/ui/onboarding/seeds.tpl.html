We share our skills, resources, and ideas by creating "seeds".

Seeds are visible to everyone in the community. There are three kinds of seeds:

Offer

Anything you have that you want to share. It could be any thing, service, knowledge, or opportunity, from your professional or personal life.

Request

Something you're looking for--again, any thing, service, knowledge, or opportunity, whether personal or professional.

Intention

A vision of something you'd like to create, that needs the support of others to become real.
